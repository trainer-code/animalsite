Gundogs can be variety of different breeds. Here we will explore some of the most common:

Labrador

Height - 56-57cms
Weight - 29-35kgs
Breathing type - Lungs
Lifespan - 10-12 years
General Appearance - Strongly built, short coupled, very active; broad in skull; broad and deep through chest and ribs; broad and strong over loins and hindquarters.

Springer Spaniel

Height - 43-48 cm
Weight - 15-25kg
Breathing type - Lungs
Lifespan - 12-15 years
General Appearance - The Welsh Springer Spaniel is a vigorous, medium-sized bird dog of happy disposition, known for versatility in the field, companionability at home, and dashing good looks anywhere. .
