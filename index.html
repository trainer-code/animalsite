Hey Kids! Welcome to this site dedicated to the wonderful animals gundogs!
On the site you'll find interesting facts, lovely pictures and all sorts! Enjoy.

The gun dog is a member of the species Canis familiaris. This species contains domesticated decendents of the wolf, and is also known as the domestic dog.

Sub-species of gun dogs include Pointers, Retrievers, Setters, Spaniels and Water dogs.
